Who am I?

My name is Bailey Leuth. I am currently a senior attending Rosemount High School. Throughout my high school career I have always strived to push myself and do the best work I can do. This carries over to taking almost exclusively Advance Placement (AP) and Honor classes starting with AP US History sophomore year with a grand total of 8 AP and 9 honors classes. Now as a senior my class load includes AP BC Calculus (equates to two semesters of college level calc), Honors Physics, AP Literature and College in Schools (CIS) Data structures.

Outside of school, I have been a part of many extra curriculars including four years on my school's cross country team, three years playing trumpet in the top jazz band of my school and three years in the Minnesota Youth Symphony. The last two years with the symphony I have been in the top orchestra and this year sit in the principal chair.
